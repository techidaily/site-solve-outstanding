\- iPad/iPad 2/**iPad 3**, **iPhone** and **iPod Touch** (using Lexcycle Stanza , Glider or iBooks on iOS 5.1),  
 \- **Sony Reader**,  
 \- Android devices (using WordPlayer, FBReader, Aldiko),  
 \- **Barnes & Noble Nook**,  
 \- Hanlin eReader,  
 \- BeBook,  
 \- Bookeen Cybook Gen3,  
 \- Cybook Opus,  
 \- iRex Digital Reader 800, 1000,  
 \- PocketBook Reader,

 \- **Kobo Vox,**
